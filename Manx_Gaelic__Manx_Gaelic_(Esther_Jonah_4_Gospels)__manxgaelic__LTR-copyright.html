Manx Gaelic (Esther, Jonah, Four Gospels)

Gaelic Scripture Portions (Manx Gaelic)

The Manx Gaelic Scripture portions were found at: http://www.gaelg.iofm.net/BIBLICAL/BIBLICAL.html. They include Esther, Jonah, and the Four Gospels.

This looks like it has been scanned in, so there are probably a few spelling errors.

Manx was spoken on the Isle of Man in Great Britain. The Ethnologue lists Manx as having no native speakers.
